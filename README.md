# Description

[#] TorrHunt is a torrent search engine android application that allows you to search and download torrents & magnet links directly from your mobile.

[#] This app saves you time by including multiple torrent sources & providers in one place.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/97ZsMJH.png" width="300">

[#] You can now find, search & download any of your favorite free and open-source content.

# Features

[#] Every result comes with important information about the torrent: title, category, size, leechers, seeders, and date.

[#] The ability to sort your results the way you want: by seeders, leechers, size, or category.

[#] Multiple torrent sources & providers in one place.

[#] The ability to browse what trending in the world

[#] Search autocomplete & suggestions, that can correct you & help you remember.

[#] Dynamic database with the latest URLs of sources and providers, with auto synchronization.

[#] The ability to open the source website, and check/audit and read comments about the torrent before downloading.

[#] Always be up to date with our "Update-notifier".

[#] Toggle, update or edit sources and and providers URLs.

[#] The ability to copy & share torrent & magnet links.

[#] Test the reachability, check if sources & providers websites are working on your network.

[#] Built-in search history.
